Professional Profile
TIMUZ is a leading interactive entertainment and game development company for the mobile world. We design, develop, test and publish a wide array of games crossing various genres like Casual, Puzzle, RPG, Skill, Arcade, Racing, Action, Adventure, Strategy etc across all platforms.
Our aim is to create games that are creative, immersive, addictive yet easy to understand. We tailor-make them for the ever competitive nature of the gaming industry while maintaining global standards and a universal appeal.
We include social features, rigid and tidy code, uncompromising QA to deliver products encompassing a high entertainment value. The bond that we share at Timuz is built purely on hard work, dedication, collaboration, passion, humility and respect.
We believe that these elements, in combination with a pinch of luck have been making our games a successful endeavor; enabling us to fuel our dream of bringing better games to you.
