Thanks for visiting
Thank you for your visit to our dynamic new concept in an ancient world! This fresh and new opportunity to create interaction between sellers, collectors, academics, and curiosity seekers who all share a passion for ancient world art, has taken an extensive amount of hard work from a devoted team and we hope that the benefits will be shared by all.
ANTICIPATED LAUNCH of Mid-April
This project has been many months in the process of development but Not Yet Perfected. Please Check back again in Late April when we expect to be Fully Operational!
The Concept
The principle here is unique in the idea that we believe education is the key to happy collecting. This theory has lead to the creation of a brilliant forum where collectors and potentially interested collectors can come to discuss their interests, view objects of high quality, acquire items that one may have never thought possible, and interact with specialists surrounding the globe so that everyone can benefit.
An idea Changing the Field
Combining the passion for collecting objects of antiquity with the modern world of cyber-space is daunting, however, at Ancientroom.com, simplicity is key. You will find the website exceptionally user friendly, easy to navigate, and something that exists no where else. As a user of this site, the ability to interact with others who share your passion is the core element; how you use this tool is up to you!
Our determination to provide an atmosphere that is user friendly and beneficial to each and every user is your advantage. The creation of Ancientroom.com has been long and tedious and the benefit is meant for its users. Here, you will find the ability to span the globe in searching for others who share your passions. Also, you have the ability to advertise and market your own objects.
Curious?
It could be that you have a few objects you believe are ancient, but you don't know where to get advice at. Here, all you need to do is post a few photographs and you will receive input from the community of people worldwide who have experience and knowledge. It's as simple as that!
Community
Ancientroom is built on a community of people with similar collecting interests. The idea comes from the fact that, sooner or later, everyone with similar interests will meet. We want to facilitate this situation so that less time is wasted! On this website, one can easily navigate between the discussion forums, galleries of dependable retail sellers who offer highest quality objects, classified advertisements for users of the site, and the live auction where objects of high quality are being offered every day.
Join The Community!
The benefits are immense. If you seek to buy, sell, or discuss ancient objects from anywhere in the world, then THIS is your place. The largest community of respected buyers and sellers for such objects exists right here and the networking possibilities are growing every day. Objects are being traded by the minute and if you hesitate, it's opportunities lost!
