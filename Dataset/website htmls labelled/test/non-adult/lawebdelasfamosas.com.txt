Kate Del Castillo Says Don’t Believe Everything Said About Her
by Latin Gossip Staff on 01.14.2016 - Kate Del Castillo
Kate del Castillo has broken her silence!
The Mexican actress is being credited with helping Sean Penn set up his “Rolling Stone” interview with Mexican drug lord Joaquin “El Chapo” Guzman, and now she is saying some of the things being reported about her aren’t true.
“Thank you for your support over the past few days. Not surprisingly, many have chosen to make up items they think will make good stories and that aren’t truthful. I look forward to sharing my story with you.”
A second tweet, sent at about the same time in Spanish, said the alleged fabrications were intended to “distract us from the real issues.”

Alejandro Gonzalez Iñarritu Gets Oscar Nomination For Best Director
by Latin Gossip Staff on 01.14.2016 - Alejandro Gonzalez Iñarritu
Another Oscar nomination!
Alejandro Gonzalez Iñarritu has been nominated in the category of Best Director for his work on “The Revenant” – which also has been nominated for Best Picture of the year.
The director has some heavy competition from Adam McKay (“The Big Short”), George Miller (“Mad Max: Fury Road”), Lenny Abramson (“Room”), and Tom McCarthy (“Spotlight”).
Alejandro won the Oscar last year with the film “Birdman”…can he do it two years in a row??!

Colombia’s El Abrazo De La Serpiente Nominated For an Oscar
by Latin Gossip Staff on 01.14.2016 - Award Shows
The 88th annual Academy Award nominations for best foreign-language film have been announced….and one of our countries is in!
Colombia’s “El Abrazo De La Serpiente” (or “Embrace of the Serpent”) received a nom for the award.
The film is up against France’s “Mustang,” Hungary’s “Son of Saul,” Jordan’s “Theeb” and Denmark’s “A War.”
The Oscars will be presented February 28 in Los Angeles.
