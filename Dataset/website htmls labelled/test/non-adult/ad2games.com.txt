Who we are
ad2games is a performance marketing platform for games. We are the largest game-focused ad-tech company in Europe and one of the leading game distribution companies worldwide.
What we do
We connect game advertisers with a vast network of publishers. We maintain a tech-focus and specialise in optimising campaigns and placements for the benefit of both!
Why choose us
Leveraging advanced behavioral targeting technologies and data processing, we can deliver the best converting games for the highest publisher payouts and the best quality traffic for our advertisers!
