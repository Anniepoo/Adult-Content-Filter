About Ephrontech And
Our Management Strategy
EphronTech is a boutique Information Technology solutions company headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area. It is our core focus to provide businesses with web, software and mobile solutions that are specific to their industry and their needs. In our ten years of serving the Bay Area, we have developed numerous lasting and fruitful relationships across a variety of businesses. We have engaged with businesses worldwide in delivering cost effective, quality solutions in a timely manner. Our execution unit is located in Coimbatore, India. While the execution of engagements are carried out by teams working from this office, our managers work locally with our clients.
Our IT solutions focus on five main areas: social media technology, enterprise integration, mobile development, custom software development and marketing services. We’ve worked in a large variety of industries such as investment, retail, accounting and litigation. We provide each client with a dedicated teams of specialists attuned to their needs.
