Who we are?
We are smart data company that reduces information clutter for consumer, providing smarter audience buying decisions for agencies, DSP/ATD/SSP platforms, publishers.
Our main product: Aidata.me - data management platform for programmatic marketing.Aidata.me aggregates, processes, segments and delivers data about age, gender, interests and intents of more than 1 000 000 000 unique cookies per month. Leading ad serving solutions use our data to deliver millions of impressions per day to their advertisers.
We created more than 2 000+ audience segments for better targeting based on online&offline data.
